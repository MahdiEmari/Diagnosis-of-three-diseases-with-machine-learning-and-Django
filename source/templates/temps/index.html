    About Us

    Welcome to our website! The disease diagnosis site was created using machine learning models in order to provide improved and accurate services in the field of diagnosis of various diseases. Our team is interested in mathematics, computer science, and medicine, and uses its capabilities in machine learning to improve algorithms and disease prediction models.

    Shahid Shamsipour Technical and Vocational College - Tehran

    Our team is led by Mehdi Amari, a final semester bachelor's student at Shahid Shamsipur Faculty of Computer Science. Our team members seek to improve diagnostic processes in medical sciences by providing innovative solutions and advanced research. Make sure that our team is ready to provide quality services and accurate information in the field of disease diagnosis using machine learning models. Trust us to help improve medical services and improve community health with your cooperation. If you have any questions or suggestions, we'd love to hear from you. Use the Contact Us page to contact us. Thank you for your kind attention, Disease Diagnosis with Machine Learning Models team
